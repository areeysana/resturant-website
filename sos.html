Sultans of Spice

    
Relaxed hotel restaurant serving dinner & buffet lunch inspired by regional Indian specialties.
Presenting Koramangala's Best Butter Chicken, Sultans of Spice has become Koramangala's most iconic
North Indian Restaurant/Bar, serving since 2010. Pop in for a spot of affordable curries, kebabs, beers, 
and beverages with a comfortable ambiance at the Rooftop of BluPetal Hotel.
